About me

Mr. Shant is a hands-on, results oriented professional equipped with 17 years of IT and business related success, experienced in software and database development, project management and ERP consultancy. Shant received his BS in Computer Science in 1999 (LAU, Beirut). After working couple of years as a sofftware developer Shant, advanced his education by doing MBA in management in 2010 ( LAU, Beirut).

Shant moved to US in 2013 and now he is working at one of well known restaurants chain corporations in California as an "Enterprise Systems Manager". Shant is also advancing his education and career by taking full stack web programming course at UCI.
